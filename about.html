About the Institute

Established on August 1, 1989, Government Industrial Training Institute (ITI) Kuchaman City is a premier vocational institute located in Nagaur district, Rajasthan. The institute offers various trades such as Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA), Electrician, Fitter, and Electronics Mechanic. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry needs by providing hands-on training, modern technical knowledge, and practical skills.

About the Website

This website has been developed as part of the academic project work for the 2024–25 session by the students of Govt ITI Kuchaman, enrolled in the Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA) trade, Unit 1. The website showcases our learning and provides essential information about the institute and its services.
